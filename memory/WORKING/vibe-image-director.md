## Creative Direction (Batch 3)

- **Photography style**: Authentic candid, NOT stock, warm natural lighting, shallow DoF
- **Typography style**: High-contrast bold for TOBI, elegant serif for quote cards, clean sans-serif for minimal
- **Key principle**: Every image must feel like something a real person would share from their phone
- **No branding**: Zero logos, watermarks, URLs — pure organic engagement aesthetic
