- [ ] Fears: 5+ items
- [ ] Beliefs: 5+ items
- [ ] Objections: 5+ items
- [ ] Emotional Triggers: 5+ items

- [ ] Ebook Angles: 3+ items
- [ ] Marketing Hooks: 3+ items
- [ ] Transformation Promise: Filled with before/after
- [ ] Awareness Stage: Stage + confidence + reasoning
