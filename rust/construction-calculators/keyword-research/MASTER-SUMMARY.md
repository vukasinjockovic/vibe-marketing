## 9. Key Strategic Insights

### Highest-Value Verticals by RPM
1. **Roofing** -- $50-65 RPM. Contractors pay $50-120/click. Even low-traffic roofing pages are goldmines.
2. **Windows & Doors** -- $35-95 CPC on estimate keywords. High-intent, replacement market.
3. **Flooring** -- $25-78 CPC on estimate keywords. Home Depot and Lowes bid aggressively.
4. **Concrete** -- $25-35 RPM. Massive volume compensates for lower CPCs.
5. **Square Footage** -- $25 RPM. Enormous volume (25M+/mo) but low CPC. Pure display ad play.

### Traffic Acquisition Strategy
- **Square footage** is the traffic gateway -- 25.8M searches/mo. Build it first, cross-link to material calculators.
- **Concrete** is the conversion engine -- 6.7M searches at higher CPC.
- **Roofing** is the revenue maximizer -- lower volume but 3-10x the RPM of other categories.
- **Long-tail pSEO** creates the moat -- 500+ pages that competitors cannot easily replicate.

### Competitive Positioning
- Most competitors (Calculator.net, OmniCalculator) have generic tools. A construction-specific site with deep material knowledge, local pricing data, and visual outputs (diagrams, material lists) can differentiate.
- Brand-variant pages (Quikrete calculator, Trex deck calculator) capture branded traffic that OmniCalculator ignores.
- "Near me" and estimate keywords signal commercial intent -- perfect for lead gen monetization alongside ads.
