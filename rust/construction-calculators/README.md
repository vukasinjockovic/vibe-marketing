# Construction Calculators

Niche calculators for trades and construction — high RPM, massive pSEO, low competition.

## Why This Wins
- **Highest RPMs** ($25-40) — visitors buy expensive materials (Home Depot, Lowes, lumber yards bid aggressively)
- **Lowest competition** of all 6 projects — no dominant player
- **Massive pSEO**: every material × every project type × every unit = 10,000+ pages
- **Rust advantage**: complex 3D calculations, real-time updates, offline PWA for job sites
- **Repeat visitors**: contractors use calculators daily on every job

## Revenue Model
- **Primary**: Display ads ($25-40 RPM — construction/home improvement is premium)
- **Secondary**: Affiliate links to material suppliers (Home Depot, Lowes, Amazon)
- **Bonus**: Offline PWA = mobile app without app store fees
