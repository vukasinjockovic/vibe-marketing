# Quotable Quotes from Expert Secrets
**Author:** Russell Brunson
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10

## On Belief and Stories

> "My goal in my marketing is to rewrite the story that's inside of people's heads."
> -- Craig Clemens, quoted in Secret #10

> "The stories you believe will form your identity and shape the direction of your life. If you change the stories you believe, you can create an identity shift and change the direction of your life."
> -- Secret #10

> "Our only real goal in any of our marketing is to identify the false beliefs and false stories our customers are telling themselves -- the ones that are keeping them from success -- and to rewrite these stories inside their minds."
> -- Secret #10

> "If I can identify people's false beliefs and tell stories that show them the truth, I don't need to 'sell' them anything. The stories lead people to the right belief, and they sell themselves."
> -- Secret #10

> "We make our purchasing decisions based on emotion, and then we use logic to justify those decisions."
> -- Secret #7

## On The Big Domino

> "If I can make people believe that [new opportunity] is the key to [desired result] and is only attainable through [my vehicle], then all other objections become irrelevant."
> -- Secret #12

> "The more things they asked someone to believe in their sales pitch, the worse the offer converted. If they tried to make more than one point or ask someone to focus on more than one thing in a sales message, conversion rates dropped by half!"
> -- Perry Belcher, quoted in Secret #12

> "The idea is to have a single point of belief that your message is built around and is emphasized over and over and over again from a variety of different angles."
> -- Jason Fladlien, quoted in Secret #12

## On Selling and Teaching

> "What I discovered from the best people in the business was that teaching the best content hurts sales. But learning how to identify, break, and rebuild false belief patterns gets people to take the action they need in order to change."
> -- Secret #11

> "When you are selling, you do not teach the tactics. You teach the what (strategy) but not the how. Your audience's desire for the how is the reason they are going to give you money at the end of your presentation."
> -- Secret #11

> "I honestly believe that the greatest service you can provide for someone is getting them to buy something. The act of buying creates a commitment that causes someone to take action."
> -- Secret #13

> "One of my early mentors, Bill Glazer, explained that I was keeping people from success because I was teaching them."
> -- Secret #13

## On The Stack and Closing

> "The only thing prospects remember when you sell is the last thing you show them."
> -- Armand Morin's Stack principle, Secret #14

> "That single concept [the Stack] has made me more money than anything else I've ever done in this business. Study it. Master it. There is no greater gift I could give you than this."
> -- Secret #14

> "Since that day I have never sold anything without using the Stack."
> -- Secret #14

> "Do not skip this part! It is the key to getting people to buy immediately. If they leave the webinar, the chances of them coming back and buying later are almost zero."
> -- On urgency/scarcity, Secret #14
