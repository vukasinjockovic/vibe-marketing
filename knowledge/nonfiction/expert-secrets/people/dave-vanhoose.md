## Summary
Selling expert who created the "If All" statement technique used in the Stack section of the Perfect Webinar. The technique involves asking "If all this package did was [result], would it be worth $[total value]?" three times (once per secret), getting three mental "yes" responses at the full 10x value before revealing the actual discounted price.
