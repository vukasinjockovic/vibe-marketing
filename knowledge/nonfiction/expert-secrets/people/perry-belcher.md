## Summary
Direct response marketer and mentor to Brunson who discovered, through analyzing 10 years of offer data, that the number of belief points in a sales message is inversely correlated with conversion. Each additional belief point beyond one cuts conversion by approximately 50%. This finding became the empirical foundation for the Big Domino / One Thing concept in Expert Secrets.

## Key Findings
1. Analyzed all offers his companies created over 10 years and found a direct relationship between number of belief points and conversion decline.
   -- Source: Expert Secrets, Secret #12

2. "If they tried to make more than one point or ask someone to focus on more than one thing in a sales message, conversion rates dropped by half!"
   -- Source: Expert Secrets, Secret #12
