# Armand Morin
**Type:** researcher (practitioner/mentor)
**Status:** partial
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** Armand

## Summary
Internet marketing veteran and stage closer who taught Russell Brunson "The Stack" -- the cumulative value-display technique that became the closing mechanism of the Perfect Webinar. Morin closed approximately 50% of a 1,000-person room on a $1,000 course, which inspired Brunson to learn his methods. He hosted BigSeminar, the first marketing seminar Brunson ever attended.

## Key Findings
1. Invented/popularized The Stack technique for stage selling.
   -- Source: Expert Secrets, Secret #14

2. Demonstrated that the last thing shown determines perceived value of the entire offer.
   -- Source: Expert Secrets, Secret #14

3. Hosted BigSeminar, where Brunson later had his first table rush and six-figure day using the Stack.
   -- Source: Expert Secrets, Secret #14
