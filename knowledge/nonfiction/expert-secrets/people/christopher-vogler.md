# Christopher Vogler
**Type:** researcher
**Status:** partial
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** Vogler

## Summary
Hollywood development executive and author of "The Writer's Journey" who adapted Joseph Campbell's 17-step mythological framework into a practical framework for screenwriting. Together with Michael Hauge, created "The Hero's 2 Journeys" audiobook. Brunson credits Vogler for encouraging creators to absorb story frameworks and then "make it your own."

## Key Findings
1. Adapted Campbell's 17-step myth structure into a practical screenwriting framework.
   -- Source: Expert Secrets, Secret #8

2. Vogler's advice to creators: "Absorb it, take notes, and pull out a piece here and there that sounds right to your observation of the world. This is all about how you perceive things as an artist, so you've got to make it your own."
   -- Source: Expert Secrets, Secret #8
