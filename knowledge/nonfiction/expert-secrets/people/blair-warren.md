## Summary
Author of "The One Sentence Persuasion Course" who distilled persuasion into 27 words: "People will do anything for those who encourage their dreams, justify their failures, allay their fears, confirm their suspicions, and help them throw rocks at their enemies." Brunson calls this the most important set of principles for building a tribe and references it throughout Expert Secrets as the foundation for movement building.
