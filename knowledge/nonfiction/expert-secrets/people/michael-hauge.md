**Type:** researcher
**Status:** partial

## Summary
Hollywood screenwriting consultant who, together with Christopher Vogler, developed the concept of "The Hero's 2 Journeys" -- the idea that every hero has both an external journey of achievement and an internal journey of transformation. This concept was recorded as an audiobook ("The Hero's 2 Journeys") and became a foundational source for Brunson's Epiphany Bridge script and story structure.

## Key Findings
1. Contributed the dual-journey concept: Journey of Achievement (external) and Journey of Transformation (internal).

2. The audiobook "The Hero's 2 Journeys" (with Vogler) was a primary source for Brunson's story framework.
