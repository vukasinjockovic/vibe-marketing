# Joe Karbo
**Type:** researcher
**Status:** partial
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** Joseph Karbo

## Summary
American direct response entrepreneur and author of "The Lazy Man's Way to Riches," one of the most successful income opportunity ads ever written. Karbo wrote his famous ad in one draft with practically no editing -- it ran for years and sold 3 million books. He attended Sugarman's seminar in 1978. A former TV host in Hollywood, Karbo had mastered direct response selling through his midnight-to-8AM show. He died in 1980 from a heart attack during a hostile TV interview.

## Key Contributions
1. Wrote one of the most successful mail order ads ever in a single draft
2. Pioneered the income opportunity ad category in mainstream publications
3. Demonstrated that incubation can produce near-perfect first drafts
