## Summary
Referenced by Sugarman as one of the "really great direct marketing copywriters" who "owned their own companies and learned over years of trial and error." Author of Breakthrough Advertising, a foundational text on copywriting theory covering awareness stages, market sophistication, and headline strategy. Mentioned alongside Ben Suarez and Gary Halbert as peers in the top tier of direct response copywriters.

## Key Findings
1. Referenced as an example of copywriters who run their own companies being superior to agency copywriters.
