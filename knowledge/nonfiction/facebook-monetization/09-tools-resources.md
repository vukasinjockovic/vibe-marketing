## 12 AI Prompts (Author's Templates)

1. **Niche Identifier** — Analyze topics against health/wealth/relationships markets
2. **Content Pillar Generator** — 3-5 pillars with 3-5 subtopics each
3. **Hook Optimizer** — 3 rewrites of a weak hook (curiosity-driven, bold, impossible to ignore)
4. **Content Idea Generator** — Ideas based on target audience pain points
5. **CTA Enhancer** — Rewrite CTA to be more persuasive/urgent
6. **Page/Group Finder** — Find competitor pages and groups with direct links
7. **Engagement Prompt** — 20 debate-starting questions for a niche
8. **Bio Builder** — 3 options under 120 characters
9. **Trend Monetization Framework** — 4-post sequence (Spark → Curiosity Gap → Controversy → Speculation)
10. **Paraphrasing** — Rewrite text avoiding plagiarism using tell-a-friend/chunking methods
11. **Cover Image Concept** — Headline, subheadline, visual direction for page cover
12. **Content Repurposer** — Reformat high-performing post into text post (123-136 chars)

## 5 Midjourney Image Prompt Templates

1. **Close-up** — `Close-up of [subject], realistic, soft lighting, rich texture, high detail, sharp focus --v 6 --ar 4:5`
2. **Mid-angle** — `Mid-angle shot of [subject], [location], natural light, photorealistic --v 6`
3. **Candid** — `Candid scene of [subject], natural light, lifestyle setting, storytelling composition --v 6`
4. **Flat lay** — `Top-down shot of [subject], realistic, vibrant, clean composition, taken with an iPhone 17 Pro, casual photo style --v 6 --ar 1:1`
5. **Reference match** — `[Subject], highly detailed, exact replica, photorealistic, same composition, same colors --iw 3 --v 6`
