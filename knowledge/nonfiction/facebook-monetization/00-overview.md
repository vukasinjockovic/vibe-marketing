# Facebook Monetization Course — Quick Reference

**Author:** Dotsokt | **Source:** EPUB, ~90 pages | **Model:** Faceless Facebook Pages + Content Monetization Program

## The Business Model

1. Create faceless Facebook pages in high-RPM niches
2. Post 6-14x/day using curated + AI-generated content
3. Grow via Page Like ads ($0.01-$0.04/like) + organic
4. Get invited to Facebook Content Monetization Program (pays monthly)
5. Supplement with blog traffic (AdSense/Mediavine) + affiliates
6. Scale: reinvest 20% of revenue, clone pages in other languages/niches

## Author's Claimed Numbers

- One page: $7,000/month
- Another page: $4,000-$4,300/month consistently
- Owns 10 monetized pages
- Facebook paid creators $2B+ in 2024
