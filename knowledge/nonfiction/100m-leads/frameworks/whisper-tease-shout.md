# Whisper-Tease-Shout Launch Method
**Type:** framework
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** WTS Launch, Whisper Tease Shout, Launch Sequence

## Summary
A three-phase launch sequence used for affiliate programs, product launches, or any significant offer introduction. Whisper (create curiosity with behind-the-scenes content), Tease (reveal value elements and build anticipation with proof), Shout (full call-to-action with urgency and scarcity). Each phase builds on the previous, creating maximum momentum at the point of the ask.

## Key Findings
1. Whisper phase generates curiosity without revealing details. Behind-the-scenes, hints, mystery.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Affiliates chapter

2. Tease phase reveals value elements and social proof. Builds anticipation through specifics.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Affiliates chapter

3. Shout phase is the full CTA with urgency and scarcity mechanisms activated.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Affiliates chapter

## Key Quotes
> "Whisper, tease, shout. That's how you launch affiliates."
