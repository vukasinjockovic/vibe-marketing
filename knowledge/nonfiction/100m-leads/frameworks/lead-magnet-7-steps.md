# Lead Magnet 7-Step Creation Framework
**Type:** framework
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** 7-Step Lead Magnet, Lead Magnet Framework, Lead Magnet Creation Process

## Summary
A seven-step sequential process for creating lead magnets that convert strangers into engaged leads. The steps move from problem selection through solution design to delivery and conversion. The core philosophy is "give away the secrets, sell the implementation" -- lead magnets should be genuinely valuable, not teaser content.

## Key Findings
1. The seven steps: (1) Pick a meaningful narrow problem, (2) Choose solution type (Reveal/Sample/One Step), (3) Choose delivery vehicle (Software/Info/Service/Physical), (4) Name and test, (5) Make easy to consume, (6) Make so good they feel guilty not paying, (7) CTA with reasons.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Lead Magnets chapter

2. Three types of lead magnets: Reveal problems they didn't know they had, Samples & Trials, One Step of a Multi-Step Process.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Lead Magnets chapter

3. Quality standard: "Give away the secrets, sell the implementation."
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Lead Magnets chapter
