**Type:** anecdote
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10

## Summary
Hormozi's origin story of starting his gym business, losing everything multiple times, and eventually building Gym Launch into a licensing model. The story illustrates the trial-and-error nature of advertising and the importance of persistence through failure. Serves as the narrative backbone of the book.

## What It Illustrates
1. Advertising always works -- it's only a matter of efficiency and volume.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Section I

2. Every method fails initially. Success comes from persistent testing and adaptation.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Section I
