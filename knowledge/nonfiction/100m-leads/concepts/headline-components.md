# Seven Headline Components
**Type:** concept
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** 7 Headline Components, Hook Elements, Attention Grabbers

## Summary
Seven elements that make headlines and hooks grab attention: Recency (what's new), Relevancy (what connects to their situation), Celebrity (notable person/entity), Proximity (what's local), Conflict (tension/controversy), Unusual (surprising/counterintuitive), Ongoing (developing/unfolding). Effective hooks use one or more of these components. A headline with none of them fails to capture attention.

## Key Findings
1. The seven components work across all content formats and platforms.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Content Part I

2. They form the Hook element of the Content Unit (Hook-Retain-Reward) framework.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Content Part I
