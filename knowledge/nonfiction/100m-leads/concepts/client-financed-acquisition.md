# Client Financed Acquisition
**Type:** concept
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** CFA, Self-Liquidating Offers, Break-Even Acquisition

## Summary
A business model principle where revenue from new customers within 30 days covers the cost of acquiring them. When acquisition is self-financing, growth requires no external capital and can scale without cash constraints. This is the bridge between "losing money" on ads and "printing money" -- the business sustains itself through the acquisition process.

## Key Findings
1. Break even on customer acquisition within 30 days to fund unlimited growth.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Paid Ads Part II

2. Pairs with LTGP:CAC ratio of 3:1 minimum for long-term profitability.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Paid Ads Part II

## Key Quotes
