# Goodwill Formula
**Type:** concept
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** Goodwill = Value - Price, Word of Mouth Driver

## Summary
Goodwill equals the gap between value delivered and price charged. The larger this gap, the more organic word-of-mouth a business generates. No referral program, incentive structure, or marketing tactic can overcome low goodwill. This is the prerequisite for all referral and affiliate strategies.

## Key Findings
1. Goodwill = Value Delivered - Price Charged.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Customer Referrals chapter

2. High goodwill is the prerequisite for referrals. No program overcomes bad product-price fit.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Customer Referrals chapter

## Key Quotes
> "If you make more value than you charge, people tell their friends."
