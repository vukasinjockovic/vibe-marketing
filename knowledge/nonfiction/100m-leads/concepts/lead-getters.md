# Lead Getters
**Type:** concept
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** Lead Getters, The Four Lead Getters, Scaling Through People

## Summary
People who do the Core Four advertising activities on your behalf, enabling scale beyond personal capacity. Four types: Customer Referrals (lowest cost exponential growth), Employees (direct influence, run business on your behalf), Agencies (teach skills you keep forever), Affiliates (operate entirely on their own). The natural progression is Referrals -> Employees -> Agencies -> Affiliates.

## Key Findings
1. Customer referrals have biggest potential for low-cost exponential growth.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Section IV

2. Employees have direct influence and run your business on your behalf.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Section IV

3. Agencies teach skills you keep forever and can transfer to your team.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Section IV

4. Affiliates, once launched, can operate entirely on their own.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Section IV
