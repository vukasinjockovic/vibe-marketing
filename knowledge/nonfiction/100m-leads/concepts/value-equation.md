# Value Equation
**Type:** concept
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** Value Formula, Hormozi Value Equation, Dream Outcome Formula

## Summary
The master formula from $100M Offers, referenced throughout $100M Leads. Value = (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay x Effort & Sacrifice). The top of the equation (increase these) drives desire. The bottom (decrease these) removes friction. The equation underpins offer construction, lead magnet design, ad copy, and all value communication.

## Key Findings
1. Four drivers: Dream Outcome (+), Perceived Likelihood (+), Time Delay (-), Effort & Sacrifice (-).
   -- Source: $100M Offers (referenced in $100M Leads throughout)

2. The best companies focus on the bottom of the equation -- making things immediate, seamless, effortless.
   -- Source: $100M Offers, referenced in $100M Leads Paid Ads chapter

3. In $100M Leads, the Value Equation is used to generate ad angles via What-Who-When.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Paid Ads Part I
