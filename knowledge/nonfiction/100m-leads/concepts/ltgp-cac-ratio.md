# LTGP:CAC Ratio
**Type:** concept
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** Lifetime Gross Profit to Customer Acquisition Cost, LTV:CAC, Profitability Ratio

## Summary
The ratio of Lifetime Gross Profit (total profit from a customer over their lifetime) to Customer Acquisition Cost (total cost to acquire that customer). Minimum healthy ratio is 3:1. Below 3:1, the advertising channel is unprofitable or the offer needs restructuring. This is the ultimate measure of advertising efficiency.

## Key Findings
1. Minimum 3:1 ratio for a healthy business.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Paid Ads Part II

2. Below 3:1 means the channel is unprofitable or the offer needs work.
   -- Source: $100M Leads, Paid Ads Part II

## Key Quotes
> "LTGP to CAC. Three to one or better."
