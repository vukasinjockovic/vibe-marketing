# Dove -- "Creams Your Skin While You Bathe"
**Type:** anecdote
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-10
**Aliases:** Dove campaign, Dove positioning, Dove soap

## Summary
Ogilvy's exemplar of positioning power. He could have positioned Dove as a detergent bar for men with dirty hands but chose to position it as a toilet bar for women with dry skin. The promise "Dove creams your skin while you bathe" won in testing. The campaign was still working 25 years later, demonstrating how the right positioning decision creates decades of advertising effectiveness.

## What It Illustrates
1. Positioning determines everything: same product, different positioning = completely different market outcome.
   -- Source: Ogilvy on Advertising, Ch.2

2. Promise testing: the specific promise was chosen because it won in research testing.
   -- Source: Ogilvy on Advertising, Ch.2

3. Longevity: still running successfully 25 years after launch.
   -- Source: Ogilvy on Advertising, Ch.2
